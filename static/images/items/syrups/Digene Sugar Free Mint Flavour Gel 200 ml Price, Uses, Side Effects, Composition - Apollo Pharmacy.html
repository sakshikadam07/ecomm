About Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml

Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml belongs to a group of medicines called 'antacids' used to treat acidity, heartburn, indigestion, gastritis (inflammation of the stomach), and stomach upset. The stomach is usually protected from the acid by a mucous layer. In some cases, the mucous layer gets eroded due to excess acid production, which leads to complications like acidity and heartburn.

Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml is a combination of four drugs: aluminium hydroxide, magnesium hydroxide, simethicone, and sodium carboxymethylcellulose. Its scientifically developed combination of active ingredients provides a high acid-neutralizing capacity property. Thus, it provides relief from acidity and gas whenever acidity strikes. It is a sugar-free syrup that provides effective action against acidity, reduces stomach pain, and protects the stomach from making excess acid for long-lasting relief.

Take Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml with or without food. Take the required dose/quantity of Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml using the measuring cup provided by the pack; shake the bottle well before each use. You are advised to take Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml for as long as your doctor has prescribed it for you, depending on your medical condition. In some cases, you may experience certain common side-effects such as constipation, loss of appetite, weakness, and diarrhoea. Most of these side effects do not require medical attention and will resolve gradually over time. However, you are advised to talk to your doctor if you experience these side effects persistently.

Inform your doctor before taking Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml if you have low phosphate levels, high magnesium levels, kidney or liver problems. Consult your doctor before taking Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml should not be given to children as safety has not been established. Avoid consuming alcohol along with Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml as it could lead to increased acidity.

Drug Interactions

Drug-Drug Interaction: Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml may have interaction with pain killers (aspirin), antibiotics (doxycycline, oxytetracycline, levofloxacin, azithromycin, ciprofloxacin), anti-asthma (albuterol, ipratropium), anti-diabetic (glipizide), and vitamins (ascorbic acid, ergocalciferol, cholecalciferol).

Drug-Food Interaction: Avoid taking citrates along with Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml as it might lead to increased serum levels of aluminium. Avoid alcohol intake as it leads to increased production of stomach acid, thereby increases acidity and heartburn.

Drug-Disease Interaction: Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml may interact with constipation, inflammatory bowel disease, intestinal obstruction disorder, and renal dysfunction.

Patients Concern

Disease/Condition Glossary

The stomach is usually protected from the acid by a mucous layer. In some cases, the mucous layer gets eroded due to excess acid production, which leads to complications like acidity. Due to this, acid frequently flows back into the food pipe (esophagus). This backflow (acid reflux) irritates the food pipe and causes heartburn. Symptoms include heartburn, sour or bitter taste in the mouth, and difficulty swallowing. Indigestion is the feeling of fullness. It occurs due to slow gastric emptying. Symptoms include stomach pain, bloating, heartburn, nausea, and vomiting.

FAQs

How does Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml work?

Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml is a combination of four drugs: aluminium hydroxide, magnesium hydroxide, simethicone, and sodium carboxymethylcellulose. Its scientifically developed combination of active ingredients provides a high acid-neutralizing capacity property. Thus, it provides relief from acidity and gas whenever acidity strikes. It is a sugar-free syrup that provides effective action against acidity, reduces stomach pain, and protects the stomach from making excess acid for long-lasting relief.

Can I use Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml for a longer period of time?

Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml should not be taken for longer periods of time unless prescribed by a doctor. Speak with your doctor if you do not feel better upon 1-2 weeks of using Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml.

Does Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml cause diarrhoea?

Diarrhoea might be a side-effect of Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml. Drink lots of fluids and eat food rich in fibre if you experience diarrhoea. If you find blood in stools (tarry stools) or if you experience excess diarrhoea, consult your doctor. Do not take anti-diarrheal medicine on your own.

Is it safe to take other medicines along with Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml?

Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml should not be taken along with other medicines as it might affect how other medicines work. To prevent this, maintain a minimum gap between both.

Can I stop taking Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml on my own?

Do not stop taking Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml on your own. Treat your condition effectually and continue taking Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml for as long as your doctor has prescribed it for you. Do not be reluctant to speak with your doctor if you experience any difficulty while taking Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml.

Can I stop taking Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml if I feel better?

No, please do not stop taking Digene Sugar Free Mint Flavour Gel 200 ml even if you feel better as it may worsen your condition. For the best advice, consult your doctor and do as recommended.
